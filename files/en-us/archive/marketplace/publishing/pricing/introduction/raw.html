Making your apps available for a payment on Firefox Marketplace is straightforward: You setup an account with one or more payment providers, set your app's price and the countries it will be sold in and if it's an upgrade to a free app promote it as the premium version. If you also use in-app payments, you need to obtain an API key and define your in-app products. This section provides a guide to setting up your paid Open Web Apps and their in-app products on Firefox Marketplace, as well as providing useful additional information.
